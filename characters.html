Major characters

Figures from the books, CD Projekt games, Netflix series, and anime.

Geralt of Rivia

Witcher of the School of the Wolf

A monster hunter with white hair and two swords. He tries to stay neutral but politics always finds him.

Ciri

Princess of Cintra, Elder Blood

Heir to Cintra with a destiny tied to other worlds and the Wild Hunt.

Yennefer

Yennefer of Vengerberg, Sorceress

A powerful mage from Vengerberg linked to Geralt through a wish and long history.

Triss Merigold

Sorceress of the Lodge

Friend to Geralt and Ciri, tied to Temeria and the fight against Nilfgaard.

Jaskier (Dandelion)

Bard and poet

Geralt's loud friend who writes ballads and gets into trouble on purpose.

Vesemir

Witcher and mentor

Oldest witcher at Kaer Morhen who trained Geralt and helps train Ciri.

Lambert

Witcher of the Wolf School

Sharp-tongued witcher who still fights beside Geralt when Kaer Morhen is threatened.

Eskel

Witcher of the Wolf School

Calm brother-in-arms to Geralt, raised and trained at Kaer Morhen.

Letho of Gulet

Kingslayer, Viper School

Bulk witcher from the School of the Viper, tied to political plots in the Northern Kingdoms.

Zoltan Chivay

Dwarf fighter and friend

Loyal companion who fights beside Geralt and trades insults with Dandelion.

Philippa Eilhart

Sorceress and Redanian court

Powerful mage and schemer behind many plots in Redania and the Lodge.

Vernon Roche

Commander of the Blue Stripes

Temerian special forces leader who crosses paths with Geralt during the war.

Iorveth

Scoia'tael commander

Elf who leads squirrel guerrillas and fights for his people in the second game.

Keira Metz

Sorceress

Former Lodge member who hides in Velen and bargains hard with Geralt.

Sigismund Dijkstra

Master of Redanian intelligence

Spy chief with a sharp mind and a body that does not slow him down.

Crach an Craite

Jarl of Skellige

Skellige ruler tied to Ciri through blood and politics.

Emhyr var Emreis

Emperor of Nilfgaard

The White Flame who leads Nilfgaard and shapes Ciri's fate in the saga.

Eredin Bréacc Glas

King of the Wild Hunt

Spectral ruler who chases Ciri across worlds in the games and books.

Regis

Emiel Regis Terzieff-Godefroy, Higher vampire

Refined vampire ally in Blood and Wine with a long past and strong honour.

Anna Henrietta

Duchess of Toussaint

Ruler of Toussaint who drags Geralt into courtly plots and monsters.

Dettlaff

Dettlaff van der Eretein, Higher vampire

Powerful vampire whose love and rage drive the Blood and Wine main story.

Olgierd von Everec

Redanian noble

Cursed noble whose pact with Gaunter O'Dimm drives Hearts of Stone.

Gaunter O'Dimm

Man of Glass

Mysterious being who trades in wishes and collects debts in cruel ways.

Cahir

Cahir Mawr Dyffryn aep Ceallach, Nilfgaardian officer

Young knight who chases Ciri in the books and appears in the Netflix show.

Fringilla Vigo

Nilfgaardian sorceress

Mage tied to Nilfgaard and Yennefer in the books and series.

Queen Calanthe

Lioness of Cintra

Ciri's grandmother, a fierce ruler who dies defending Cintra.

Mousesack

Druid

Skellige druid who protects and guides figures tied to destiny in adaptations.

Renfri of Creyden

Princess turned bandit

Central to the Lesser Evil tale in the books and the first season of the show.

Avallac'h

Aen Elle sage

Elven mage who guides Ciri among the Aen Elle in the saga.

Vilgefortz of Roggeveen

Mage and warrior

Charismatic sorcerer whose true plans drive major book and series conflict.
