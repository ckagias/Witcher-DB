The saga in print

Andrzej Sapkowski’s Witcher stories began as short fiction and grew into a five-novel arc, plus prequels and collections. Below are the main book editions in reading order for the core saga. Use the # column as a starting guide for the main arc if you are new to the series.

Games and TV adaptations follow these characters. This page stays focused on the books so the written canon is easy to scan. For characters, events, and places in other media, check Characters and Timeline.

Main volumes

# Cover Title Kind Year Notes
1 The Last Wish Short story 1993 Introduces Geralt and the tone of the series
2 Sword of Destiny Short story 1992 Stories that lead into the novel saga
3 Blood of Elves Novel 1994 First novel of the five-part main saga
4 Time of Contempt Novel 1995 Thanedd coup and broken alliances
5 Baptism of Fire Novel 1996 Geralt’s company on the trail to find Ciri
6 The Tower of the Swallow Novel 1997 Ciri’s parallel paths and the tower symbol
7 Lady of the Lake Novel 1999 Finale of the novel saga
8 Season of Storms Novel 2013 Midquel set between early stories
