The Witcher on Screen

Three adaptations on Netflix: one live-action series and two animated films.

The Witcher

2019–present Live action Netflix 4 seasons ★ 7.8/10

Geralt of Rivia hunts monsters while fate ties him to a sorceress and a child of prophecy. Opens in the tone of the short stories, then moves into the saga.

Official trailer

Main cast

  • Geralt of Rivia Henry Cavill (S1–S3), Liam Hemsworth (S4+)
  • Yennefer of Vengerberg Anya Chalotra
  • Ciri Freya Allan
  • Jaskier Joey Batey

Timeline & books

Geralt of Rivia, a solitary monster hunter, struggles to find his place in a world where people often prove more wicked than beasts.

Nightmare of the Wolf

2021 Animated film Studio Mir 81 min ★ 7.2/10

A prequel to the live-action series: a young Vesemir survives the Trial of the Grasses and the politics that made witchers both necessary and feared.

Official trailer

Main characters

  • Vesemir Theo James
  • Tetra Gilcrest Lara Pulver
  • Deglan Graham McTavish

Timeline & books

Escaping from poverty to become a witcher, Vesemir slays monsters for coin and glory, but when a new menace rises, he must face the demons of his past.

Sirens of the Deep

2025 Animated film Studio Mir 87 min ★ 6.1/10

On the coast, Geralt untangles killings linked to humans and the merpeople — drawn from the short story A Little Sacrifice.

Official trailer

Main characters

  • Geralt of Rivia Witcher for hire
  • Jaskier Companion
  • Agloval Client nobleman
  • Sh’eenaz Merwoman

Timeline & books

Hired to probe seaside village attacks, mutant monster hunter Geralt unravels an age-old conflict between humans and sea people that threatens war between kingdoms. Aided by allies, he must solve the mystery before hostilities escalate.
