Conjunction of the Spheres

A cosmic catastrophe that merged many worlds. Humans arrived; monsters appeared; elder races lost ground. Almost every conflict in the saga traces back to this event.

  • Aftermath on the Continent
    • Displacement of elves and dwarves
    • Spread of magical “chaos” and new creatures
    • Human kingdoms and later empires (e.g. Nilfgaard)
⚡

Elder Blood

A latent magical lineage tied to elven prophecy, concentrated in rare descendants. It drives much of the political and personal stakes around Ciri.

  1. In-universe views
    • Elven prophecies of a world-ending or world-saving child
    • Human rulers who hunt or exploit carriers
    • Sorcerers who study or fear the gene line
⚔

Witcher trials

Grueling mutations and training that give witchers enhanced reflexes, longevity, and resistance — at a brutal survival rate.

  • School of the Wolf (Kaer Morhen)
    • Herb trials, viral strains, and combat drills
    • Signs taught as controlled bursts of chaos
  • Other schools (Cat, Griffin, Bear, Viper…) follow similar myths with different reputations.
🔥

Signs & magic

Witcher Signs are simple combat spells. True sorcery is studied for decades at academies and shaped by politics through the Brotherhood and Lodge.
