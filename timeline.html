Timeline of major events

The Continent’s history is contested even in-universe.

Before recorded history

Conjunction of the Spheres

Many worlds collide, monsters appear on the Continent, humans arrive and displace elder races such as elves and dwarves.

Approx. 1,200 years before the games

Founding of Nilfgaard

A small city-state in the deep south begins its rise toward becoming the most powerful empire the Continent has ever known.

Several centuries before the games

The Founding of Kaer Morhen

Witchers establish their mountain stronghold in Kaedwen as a base for training and research.

~1230s

The Pogroms of Mages

Fearful rulers begin persecuting sorcerers, leading to the formation of the Brotherhood of Sorcerers as a political shield.

~1239 — First Northern War

Nilfgaard Invades

Emperor Emhyr var Emreis launches the first large-scale northern campaign, shocking the kingdoms.

~1263 — First Northern War

Slaughter of Cintra

Nilfgaard destroys Cintra. Queen Calanthe dies defending her city. Ciri flees north, setting the main story in motion.

~1263

Geralt Claims the Child of Surprise

At Cintra, Geralt invokes the Law of Surprise and is bound to Ciri, though he initially refuses the bond.

~1267 — Second Northern War

Battle of Sodden Hill

Mages of the north sacrifice themselves to stop Nilfgaard's advance. Yennefer is among the survivors, scarred by the battle.

~1268

The Battle of Brenna

Northern armies led by Natalis and Reuven decisively defeat Nilfgaard, halting the Second War and redrawing the map.

~1272 — Witcher 3 era

The Wild Hunt Pursues Ciri

The spectral riders cross worlds hunting Ciri for her Elder Blood, threatening to unleash the White Frost on all worlds.

~1272

Geralt Searches the War-Torn North

While Redania and Nilfgaard fight for supremacy, Geralt follows every lead to find Ciri before the Hunt does.

~1272 — Blood and Wine

Toussaint and the Unseen Elder

Geralt travels to the fairy-tale duchy of Toussaint, confronting ancient vampire politics and his own legacy.

Post-games — possible endings

Ciri's Fate

Depending on choices made, Ciri either becomes a Witcher, ascends to rule Nilfgaard, or vanishes into another world entirely.
